# Product Vision: Aquascape Vision

## Overview

Aquqscape Vision is a planning and visualization application for aquarists who build planted aquariums. The application aims to help hobbyists design, evaluate, and refine aquqscapes before investing in plants, hardscape materials, livestock, and equipment. <br />

The long-term vision is to provide aquarists with a digital environment where they can experiment with layouts, explore plant combinations, visualize growth over time, and make informed purchasing decisions with confidence. <br />

Aquascape Vision seeks to reduce the uncertainty, cost, and frustration associated with designing planted aquariums while encouraging creativity and succesful outcomes.

---

## Problem Statement

Designing a planted aquarium requires hobbyists to make numerous decisions before they can see the final result. Aquascapers must choose plants, hardscape materials, and layout styles based largely on experience, photographs, and trial and error. <br />

Several common challenges exist:

### Difficulty Visualizing the Final Aquarium

Plants are often purchased when small and immature. Hobbyists struggle to predict how an aquascape will look weeks or months later as plants grow, spread, and compete for space.

### Costly Mistakes

Plants, driftwood, rocks, substrates, and equipment can represent a significant investment. Poor planning can result in unnecessary purchases, redesigns, and replacements.

### Limited Planning Tools

Most aquascapers currently rely on sketches, photographs, spreadsheets, online forums, or generic design tools. Existing aquarium applications often focus on maintenance, livestock tracking, or water parameters rather than aquascape design.

### Knowledge Fragmentation

Information about aquatic plants is scattered across forums, websites, retailer catalogs, videos, and social media. Hobbyists must often consult multiple sources before making decisions.

### Uncertainty During Redesigns

Even experienced aquascapers may hesitate to modify established aquariums because they cannot easily preview the results of moving plants, changing hardscape, or adopting a new layout style.

---

## Target Audience

Aquascape Vision is intended for freshwater planted aquarium enthusiasts across a range of experience levels.

### Beginner Aquascapers

New hobbyists who need guidance selecting plants, understanding placement, and avoiding common design mistakes.

### Intermediate Aquascapers

Aquarists seeking to improve the appearance of their tanks, experiment with new layouts, and make better-informed purchasing decisions.

### Advanced Aquascapers

Experienced hobbyists who want a faster and more efficient way to prototype layouts, evaluate design concepts, and plan large or competition-oriented projects.

### Aquarium Retailers and Professionals

In the long term, the platform may also support retailers, designers, and service providers who wish to help customers visualize projects before making purchases.

---

## Vision

Aquascape Vision will become the preferred planning platform for planted aquarium design. <br />

Users will be able to:

- Create digital representations of aquariums.
- Arrange plants, driftwood, rocks, and decorative elements.
- Explore different aquascaping styles and layouts.
- Access a comprehensive aquatic plant knowledge base.
- Predict how a layout may evolve as plants mature.
- Experiment freely before committing to purchases or physical changes.

Over time, the platform may expand to include advanced capabilities such as growth simulation, augmented reality visualization, shopping integration, and personalized design recommendations.

---

## Why Aquascapers Will Use Aquascape Vision

Aquascapers invest significant time, effort, and money into creating beautiful planted aquariums. They want confidence that their decisions will produce the results they envision. <br />

Aquascape Vision provides value by helping users:

- Visualize layouts before purchasing materials.
- Reduce costly mistakes and unnecessary purchases.
- Explore creative ideas without risk.
- Learn about aquatic plants and their growth habits.
- Plan tank rescapes more efficiently.
- Design aquariums with greater confidence.

Rather than replacing creativity, the application serves as a digital planning companion that helps aquascapers transform ideas into successful aquascapes.

---

## Long-Term Goal

The long-term goal of Aquascape Vision is to become the most trusted digital planning and visualization platorm in the planted aquarium hobby, empowering aquascapers to design with confidence and bring their ideas to life before a single plant is purchased or placed.
